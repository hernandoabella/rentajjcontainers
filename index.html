WHO WE ARE

Boxhub Cargo Containers Trading LLC is a leading provider of innovative and reliable container solutions in the UAE and across the region. We specialize in container trading, leasing, customization, and logistics, offering end-to-end services to meet the diverse needs of our clients. WHAT WE PROVIDE

Container Trading

At Boxhub Cargo Containers Trading LLC, we offer a wide range of high-quality shipping containers for sale to meet your diverse needs.

Container Conversions

BOX HUB provides best-in-class container conversion and modification. Our professional team includes Architects, AutoCAD technician, Skilled labors. We use advanced prefabrication methods for converting containers.

International Leasing

We own 1000+ containers for domestic short and long term leasing to serve a variety of storage and shipping purposes. Both dry and refrigerated containers are included in our collection.

Domestic Leasing

Tamed containers are rented out to our domestic customers in UAE. Our domestic leasing division serves the largest fleets of customs duty paid containers to meet a spectrum of requirements.
